FAQ

Quick answers for travelers and locals.

What is a “Dude”?

A Dude is a verified local companion who can help you navigate the city, plan stops, and stay confident while exploring.

How fast can I book?

The MVP booking journey is designed to complete in five quick steps: Home → Trip Setup → Available Dudes → Booking → Payment & Confirmation.

Is payment secure?

Yes — checkout is designed around secure payments and a clear confirmation state after payment.

Which cities are supported?

City support will expand with verified Dude availability. Request access and tell us where you’re traveling.
